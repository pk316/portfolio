About me

After obtaining a degree in business economics, I began working in property management and commercial real estate. After a few years, I began to feel stagnant and no longer developing as an individual. I wanted something more challenging, rewarding and meaningful in my life. I felt like this was the perfect opportunity to pursue something that I was always interested in and excited about, web development. After expending through countless free online resources to learning web development, I decided to fully immerse myself and enrolled at LearningFuze, an accelerated web development program, in which I focused heavily on front end development. While at LearningFuze, I spent 10-12 hour days learning, programming, collaborating and problem solving with like-minded individuals. Having completed the program, I left with multitude of skills that I will be able to apply and expand upon.

Aside from web development, I enjoy traveling and being able to explore and learn from different cultures and lifestyles to give me different perspectives on life. I also have a love for sneakers. As a kid, I always loved shoes, but by high school, they became my obsession. I began investing, buying, trading and reselling highly sought after shoes to feed my sneaker addiction. Lastly, I love to learn. One can never learning enough, and that's what I love about programming; there's always something new to learn, or ways to improve your skills.
